# Computational services

Using a workstation powered by a Ryzen 7 CPU, 32GB of RAM, and a graphics card with 16GB of VRAM, I offer services focused on:

1. Tabulated transcriptions using OpenAI's [*whisper*](https://openai.com/index/whisper/) model —one of its few open-source services— enabling high-quality speech-to-text conversions.

2. Utilization of open-source Large Language Models (LLMs) for generative tasks such as text summarization, translation, abstract concept identification, and more. Scalable models like [Llama](https://www.llama.com/), [DeepSeek](https://es.wikipedia.org/wiki/DeepSeek), and [Mistral] (https://mistral.ai/en) are employed to ensure efficiency and adaptability.

3. Use of [fine-tuned](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fine-tuning_(deep_learning)) [BERT-based](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BERT_(language_model)) models for text classification tasks.
