
# Recommended bibliography

The following is a bibliography on various topics that I have found particularly productive and/or stimulating.

## On society and social sciences
1. Luhmann N.(2007) *La sociedad de la sociedad*. México, Universidad Iberoamericana.
2. Zürn, M. (2018). *A theory of global governance: Authority, legitimacy, and contestation*. Oxford University Press.
3. Esposito, E. (2022). *Artificial communication: How algorithms produce social intelligence*. MIT Press.

## About statistics and data science
1. Stock, J. H., y Watson, M. W. (2020). *Introduction to econometrics*. Pearson.
2. Hastie, T., Friedman, J. y Tibshirani, R. (2009). *The elements of statistical learning: data mining, inference, and prediction*. Springer
3. Sarkar, D., Bali, R., y Sharma, T. (2018). *Practical machine learning with Python*. Springer
